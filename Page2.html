At your infant’s mirror stage(Lacan),
You see your reflection.
At your adult’s stage(Hyper Island),
You have to do a lot of reflections!


Maybe years later, to do reflections will become
our Hypers’ collective unconscious(Jung).











“The fish here are small,
Thereby less anglers came.”


A tiny leaf of boat,
Only you and me;
To avoid the crowd,
Only river and sky.









“Set aside the dark clouds,
Pick a star together with you. ”


The happiest thing in the world
Maybe is to meet your “other” cat,
And do all the silly things together,
Through all the summers and winters.


Wish all of you find your "other" cat!
